Lasagna

Just Like Grandma's

Indulge in the comforting embrace of homemade lasagna with this timeless recipe that promises layers of rich flavors and satisfying textures. Perfect for family gatherings, cozy dinners, or just a comforting meal after a long day, this lasagna will become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 1 lb spicy italian sausage (sub sweet sausage for mild sauce)
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 cup carrots (shredded or thin sliced)
  • 4 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 2 cans san marzano tomato
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon rosemary
  • red pepper faleks to taste (optional)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • lasagna noodles
  • 1 large egg
  • cottage cheese
  • shredded parmesean cheese
  • shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

Sauce

  1. Precut your vegetables: onion (diced), carrots (shredded/thin sliced), garlic (minced). Precut sausage if in links
  2. Place large saucepot on stove on medium to high heat.
  3. Drizzle some olive oil on bottom of pan, then shuffle pan around to cover bottom in oil.
  4. Add Sausage just before oil begins to smoke. Monitor heat and try to keep it around this temperature.
  5. Cook sausage until it is brown and crispy, and fond develops on the bottom of the pot.
  6. Remove sausage from pot and place in a bowl to the side. Keep oil in pot.
  7. Sautee onion and carrots until onion begins to turn translucent.
  8. Add garlic, a pinch or two of salt, and pepper. Mix and sautee till aromatic.
  9. Deglaze with wine.
  10. Turn down heat to medium.
  11. Add canned tomatoes and tomato puree.
  12. Add 1 can equivalent of water
  13. Add sausage
  14. Add seasonings
  15. Mix thoroughly.
  16. Turn down heat to low. Get sauce to a relaively active simmer, letting the added water evaporate, and letting every ingredient get to know each other.
  17. Let simmer till sauce covers the back of the spoon.

Lasagna

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Prepare add egg to cottage cheeze and mix.
  3. Shred mozzarella and parmesean cheese.
  4. Layer cookpan: sauce, noodle, 3 cheese blend (cottage cheese mix, mozzarella, parmesean).
  5. Repeat layers till pan is entirely full. Look to get around 4 layers of noodles.
  6. Cover pan with aluminum foil.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes.
  8. Remove foil from pan. Bake for an additional 10-15 minutes.
