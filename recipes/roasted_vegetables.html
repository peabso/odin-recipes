Crispy and Delicous Vegetables

Elevate your dining experience with the tantalizing aroma and exquisite flavors of roasted vegetables. This vibrant medley of seasonal produce, kissed with the warmth of the oven and enhanced by aromatic herbs and spices, promises a symphony of taste and texture that will leave your taste buds dancing with delight.

Ingredients

  • Any vegetable you would like.
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Prepare your vegetables.
  3. Spread vegetables across baking sheet
  4. Drizzle olive oil until vegetables are coated. Do not soak vegetables in too much oil.
  5. Add seasonings to taste
  6. Pace in oven at temp. Bake for 30-45 minutes. Continuously mix vegetables every 10 minutes.
