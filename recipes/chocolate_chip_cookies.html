Delicous Cookies

Embark on a journey of sweet indulgence with this classic chocolate chip cookie recipe. Delightfully chewy on the inside, with a golden crispiness on the edges and bursts of gooey chocolate in every bite, these cookies are a timeless treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix butter, brown sugar, and sugar thoroughly and completely.
  3. Add egg yolks and mix.
  4. Add vanilla and mix.
  5. Add salt and baking soda, mix.
  6. Add flour and mix.
  7. Add desired amount of chocolate and mix.
  8. Roll out into 1 - 1 1/4 inch balls
  9. Bake at temperature for 11-16 minutes. Shorter time for gewey cookies. Longer time for crispier cookies.
