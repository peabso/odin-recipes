Mixed Berry Smoothie

Refreshing and Light

Transport yourself to a sunny morning with the vibrant hues and refreshing flavors of this Mixed Berry Smoothie. Bursting with the goodness of assorted berries, this energizing blend offers a delightful balance of sweetness and tanginess, making it the perfect way to start your day or revitalize your afternoon.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Mixed Berries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt (preferably vanilla or plain)
  • 1/2 cup milk (any milk is fine)
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • some ice cubes

Directions

  1. Blend everything until uniform.
  2. Enjoy.
